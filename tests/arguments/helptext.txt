$prog$ [options] [arguments]

ARGUMENTS
${[X,Y,Z]       | default: 0,0,-99.0}$ A comma-separated value
${[PATHS]       | delimiter: :}$ A colon-separated list
${[INT]         | valuetype: int}$ An integer
${[STRING]      }$ A string

OPTIONS
${-h, --help    }$ Show help
${--            }$ Marks the end of options
